Skydive Finger Lakes

  Ovid Airport (D82), Ovid, NY

2562 Parish Rd
Ovid NY 14521-9540 US

25 miles miles   NW of Ithaca
6 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivefingerlakes.com
  (315) 220-0052
  info@skydivefingerlakes.com
  open map
  Eastern Region
  1 Cessna 182

Open Wednesday-Monday at the drop zone-owned Ovid Airport. Located in the Finger Lakes region of New York state with wineries, state parks, swimming, boating, hiking, and fishing nearby. Skydivers at all levels are welcome. Indoor packing. Cook-outs on Saturdays. No hook turns please.
