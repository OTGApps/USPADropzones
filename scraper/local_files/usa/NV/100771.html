Welcome to Las Vegas' premier student skydiving center specializing in student tandem training. We fly a new P-750 XSTOL, utilize the newest state-of-the-art equipment, and offer complimentary transportation from the Las Vegas Strip in our luxury passenger vans. Enjoy spectacular views of Hoover Dam and Lake Mead as your friends and family watch you land right next to our facility. Your experience at Skydive Las Vegas will be second-to-none!
