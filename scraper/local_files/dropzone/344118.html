Brown DZ

  Hearne Municipal Airport, Hearne, Texas

Crennan Lane
Hearne TX 77859 US

22 miles NW of Bryan
1.6 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.idis-corp.com
  877-800-8307
  info@idis-corp.com
  open map
 
  1 Casa 212

IDIS offers 3 training hangars in Hearne, TX. Each hangar is 3150 ft, 2 with 2,230 ft and 2 with padded pack space and a double locked climate controlled gear loft or reception area. Each hangar also includes 2 debriefing stations with high definition TV's, tables, chairs, a refrigerator, coffee maker and microwave. On airport DZ's are within walking distance of all 3 hangars to simplify and streamline training efficiency, in addition to several off-airport DZ's in the surrounding area. Hearne Municipal Airport is also conveniently located within 10 minutes of commercial lodging and dining.
