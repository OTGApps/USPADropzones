Parachutisme Atmosphair

  Pintendre, Levis, Quebec/Canada

438 Chemin Ville-Marie
Lewis QC G6C 1B5 Canada

10 km S of Quebec
to nearest hotel


  https://atmosphair.com

  atmosphair@atmosphair.com
  open map
 
 

Bonjour! We are a friendly, turbine DZ located in Levis (south shore of Quebec City). Our legendary "smiley faces" yellow Caravan will take you to 14,000' ASL from May to October. Come and enjoy the breathtaking view of Quebec City, Levis, the St-Lawrence River, lle D'Orleans, Montmorency Falls and much more! CSPA or USPA membership required. Please contact us prior to your visit to confirm our daily operations: (418) 834-7272 or atmosphair@atmosphair.com. Blue Skies!
