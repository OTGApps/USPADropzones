Skydive Airtight

  Skiatook Municipal Airport (2F6), Skiatook, OK

1651 S Lombard Ln
Skiatook OK 74070-1759 US

20 miles N of Tulsa
1 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveairtight.com
  (918) 396-PULL
  skydiveairtight@cox.net
  open map
  Southwest Region
  1 Cessna 182

Open seven days a week. From Tulsa, take Hwy 75 North to state Hwy 20. Turn left(west) on Hwy 20 and go four miles to Skiatook. In Skiatook from the intersection of Hwy 11 and Hwy 20 go west on Hwy 20 to Lombard Lane. Walmart is on the NE corner of Hwy 20 and Lombard Lane. Turn south(left) one mile to 1651 S Lombard Lane. Last hanger on south end of airport. AAD REQUIRED!
