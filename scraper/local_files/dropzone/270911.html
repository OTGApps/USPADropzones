Skydive Transilvania

  Aerodrom Luncani, Luncani, Romania

Soseaua E 60 FN
Sat Luncani, Comuna Luna 
Jud. Cluj 407362 Romania

50 km S of Cluj
10 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivetransilvania.ro
  40(723)225609
  doru@skydivetransilvania.ro
  open map
 
  2 Cessna 182

DZ is in the heart of Transilvania. Open weekends; weekdays by appt. Close to tourist excursions (Salt Mines and Sibiu). Facilities for skydiver and non-skydiver (fishing pond, outside bar, small beach for swimming, park for kids). Disco on weekend with loads of locals and fun!
