Skydive San Joaquin Valley

  DB Cooper Field, Bakersfield, CA

12112 Copus Rd
Bakersfield CA 93313-9659 US

75 miles N of Los Angeles
5 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivesanjoaquin.net
  (661) 858-2972
  skydivesanjoaquin@wildblue.net
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 182

Open Friday-Sunday & most Holidays. Conveniently close to I-5 freeway. Take I-5 north out of Los Angeles. Go over the Grapevine into the Central San Joaquin Valley. Stay on I-5 to Copus Road exit. Turn left on Copus Road. DZ is less than 2 miles west on the right. King Air & Cessna 182. Clean facilities with lots of room and friendly, helpful crew.
