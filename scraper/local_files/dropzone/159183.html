16111 FM 521 Rd
Rosharon TX 77583-6821 US

25 miles S of Houston
12 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.houston.skydivespaceland.com
  (281) 369-3337
  manifesthou@SkydiveSpaceland.com

  1 Super Twin Otter, 1 C-208 900 Supervan

Park setting on 130 wide-open acres. 30 miles to Galveston Beaches. 30,000-sq.-ft. facility, indoor air-conditioned packing. Take Hwy. 288 S. from Houston to FM 1462 (Alvin, Rosharon) exit.
