Skydive Perris

  Perris Valley Airport (L65), Perris, CA

2091 Goetz Rd
Perris CA 92570-9315 US

40 miles SE of Ontario Airport
1 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveperris.com
  (951) 657-3904
  manifest@skydiveperris.com
  open map
  Western Region
  5 Skyvans, 3 Super Twin Otters, 1 DC-9

Club Med of Skydiving! Wind tunnel on-site. Open 365 days a year. Free load organizing for all disciplines on weekends. Facilities include: fully equipped house, bar, and video rooms. Indoor Skydiving Center, Square One and full rigging services on DZ. 1.5 hour drive to LAX airport; beaches, mountains, amusement parks and Hollywood. A true family vacation combination!
