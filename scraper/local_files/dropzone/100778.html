Skydive Perris

  Perris Valley Airport (L65), Perris, CA

2091 Goetz Rd
Perris CA 92570-9315 US

90 miles E of Los Angeles
1 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveperris.com
  (951) 657-3904
  manifest@skydiveperris.com
  open map
 
  5 Skyvans, 3 Super Twin Otters

Club Med of Skydiving with a Wind Tunnel in the parking lot! Open 364 days a year, complementary load organizing for all disciplines on the weekends and even most weekdays, most of the year. Our fleet of aircraft includes 3 Twin Otters, 5 Skyvans and soon-- a DC-9! In addition to blue skies year round, our facilities include a restaurant, full-bar, fully equipped bunkhouse, onsite rigging with Expanding Horizons Rigging Loft and Gold State Gear pro shop. 45 minutes from Ontario Airport, 1.5 hour drive to LAX Airport, beaches, mountains, amusement parks and Hollywood. A true family vacation combination and did we mention a lot of Blue Skies!
