Aerograd Kolomna

  Korobcheevo Airdrome, Kolomna, Russia

Korobcheevo Airfield
Kolomna District 
Moscow Region 14090 Russian Federation

100 km SE of Moscow
1 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.aerograd.ru
  7 495 746 73 80
  aerograd@aerograd.ru
  open map
 
  3 L-410 Turbolet, 1 Mi-8 helicopter

Aerograd is located on the bank of River Oka in the vicinity of the picturesque ancient town of Kolomna and about 10c from Moscow. We are only 1,5-2 hour drive from Moscow. Aerograd has an area of 250 hectares and has a capacity of up to 1,000 guests. On the territory there is the best store of parachute equipment and they also rent the most modern skydive equipment.
