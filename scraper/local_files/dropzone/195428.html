Flight - 1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses at Skydive Spain Canopy Course

  10/12/2019 - 10/13/2019

  Skydive Spain   Sevilla

   Armando Fattoruso, Course Director
Armando Fattoruso, Main Contact

 Armando@Flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Armando Fattoruso will be teaching Flight-1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses at Skydive Spain on the 12th & 13th of October 2019. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 12 October Flight 102 - Sunday 13 October To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3610 102 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3611
