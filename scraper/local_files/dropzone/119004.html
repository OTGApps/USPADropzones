SIS Boogie

  06/26/2020 - 06/28/2020

  Skydive Indianapolis   Frankfort Indiana

   Nic Tacker, Main Contact

 nic.tacker@gmail.com

 https://facebook.com/events/s/sisters-in-skydiving-sis-boogi/436501693910354/?ti=icl

Skydive Indianapolis is proud to announce we will be hosting a SIS (Sisters in Skydiving) Event!!! One of the great things about skydiving is all of the experience available to us from other jumpers, even so, it's sometimes easy to feel intimidated or even alienated as a new female jumper, especially at male dominated dropzones but SIS events like this are perfect for those of you who want to have a great time in the sky by jumping with, and learning from other ladies of the sport. Come out and join this event and become a SIS yourself. Emily Bombardi of the SCZ ZEUS Skydive Team will be our event organizer. She has organized and jumped at many events like this throughout the US. Early Bird Registration: $25.00 (Now thru May 10th 2020) After May 10th 2020: $35.00 (This will cover costs for organizer and items for the event) Jump Tickets $20
