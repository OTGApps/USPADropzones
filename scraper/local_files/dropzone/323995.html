Skydive Mossel Bay

  Mossel Bay Airfield, Mossel Bay, South Africa

Mossel Bay Airfield
69 Rooikat ST Aalwyndal
Mossel Bay 6500 South Africa

6.4 km NW of Mossel Bay
8 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivemosselbay.com
  27 82 824 8599
  hangar@skydivemosselbay.com
  open map
  Foreign
  4 Cessna 182, 2 King Air 90

Skydive Mossel Bay is situated on the beautiful Garden Route, halfway between Cape Town and Port Elizabeth. We are blessed with great weather all year round. We are open 7 days a week with permanent coach, AFF and tandem staff. We have a King Air 90 (14, 000ft) and 3 Cessna 182's for jumps 10,000ft and lower.
