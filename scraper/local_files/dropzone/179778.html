Skydive Charlevoix

  Charlevoix Municipal Airport, Charlevoix, MI

111 Airport Dr
Charlevoix MI 49720-8904 US

37 miles N of Traverse City
1/4 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.SkydiveCharlevoix.com
  (231) 242-8822
  info@skydiveharborsprings.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 182

Jump in beautiful Northern Michigan at the Charlevoix Municipal Airport. Enjoy amazing views of Grand Traverse Bay, the Beaver Island Archipelago, Little traverse Bay ans entire tip of the Mitt from as high as two miles above the ground. We specialize in first time tandems jumpers but experienced jumpers are also welcome with advance notice.
