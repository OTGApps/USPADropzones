Flight - 1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses at Skydive Sussex Canopy Course

  09/07/2019 - 09/08/2019

  Skydive Sussex   Sussex New Jersey

   Kaz Sheekey, Course Director
Kaz Sheekey, Main Contact

 Kaz@flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Kaz Sheekey will be teaching Flight 101 & 102 canopy courses at Skydive Sussex on 7th & 8th of September 2019. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Whether this is your first canopy course or if it's been a while since you gave some love to the nylon above your head, refresh your knowledge and get reacquainted with your canopy. This course is not only for newly licensed skydivers, but it's also for the experienced skydiver who is looking to refresh and refine your canopy flying skills! You will learn the fundamental skills needed to pilot your canopy safely, and practice drills that you will utilize throughout your skydiving life. AND...THAT'S NOT ALL!! NEED YOUR B-LICENSE? Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 7 September Flight 102 - Sunday 8 September Cost per course is $150 plus 5 hop n pops. To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3666 102 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3667
