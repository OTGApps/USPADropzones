Ozarks Skydive Center

  Kingsley Field (MO9), Miller, MO

3388 Lawerence 1070
Miller MO 65707 US

30 miles W of Springfield
15 miles to nearest hotel


  http://ozskydive.com
  (417) 882-5867
  ozarksskydive@gmail.com
  open map
  Central Region
  1 Cessna 182

The Midwest's friendliest drop zone. Open weekends year round and weekdays by appointment. On a private airport with plenty of packing and landing area for all experience levels. Located halfway between Springfield and Joplin. 12 miles north of Exit 38 on I-44 (2 miles north of Hwy 96) on Hwy 97 North to Lawrence County 2040, east 3/4 mile to drop zone.
