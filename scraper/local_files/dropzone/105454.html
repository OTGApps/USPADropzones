Flight - 1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses Canopy Course

  09/21/2019 - 09/22/2019

  Skydive City Zephyrhills   Zephyrhills FL

   Aaron Stocum, Main Contact

 Aaron@Flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Aaron Stocum will be teaching Flight-1 101 & 102 canopy courses at Skydive City on the 21st & 22nd of September 2019. PD Bullseye is coming to Skydive City on October 12th! This is a tournament style sport accuracy series happening across the US that will lead to a final event with the opportunity to compete to become a PD Sponsored Athlete. A canopy course is a great way to prepare for this event. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module, you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 21 September Flight 102 - Sunday 22 September Cost per course is $150 plus 5 hop n pops. To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3856 102 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/3857
