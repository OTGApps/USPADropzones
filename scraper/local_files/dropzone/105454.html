Flags in the Air, a Skydivers Tribute to 9/11

  09/11/2021 - 09/11/2021

  Skydive City Zephyrhills   Zephyrhills Florida

   Patrick W Fortune, Main Contact

 813-677-0919

 Patrick_W_Fortune@yahoo.com

This event is to honor and pay tribute to those who lost their lives on that morning 20 years ago and changed how America and Americans see the world. The event will be held at Skydive City, Zephyrhills Florida on September 11th, 2021. It will be starting at approximately 0800. We are hoping that this event will be held at every skydiving facility across America. We would like to invite all the law enforcement, first responders, firefighting personnel, and military to the skydiving facility or Drop Zone so that the Skydiving Community can show the appreciation we have for the difficult job they do each and every day. This event is open to the general public. It is not about us skydivers. It is about those people who went to work that day expecting to come home and didn’t. It is about those First Responders who arrived on scene and did their job. Did their jobs going up onto the towers knowing they were more than likely not coming back out alive. It is about those on Flight 93 who knew their plane was gong to kill more people on the ground and decided to not let that happen. They gave their own lives so that others would live. This is what the Memorial Jump on September 11th is all about. This event is not about politics. It is not about Democrats or Republicans or any other political party. This event is not about social agendas or issues. My VISION of this event: Have as many Flags in the Air as safely as possible. Added in Red, White, and Blue streamers flown by Flockers. Red and Blue smoke flown by swoopers, angle fliers, and wingsuiters. The final tribute to 9/11 will be the streamers and smoke escorting the big United States flags to the landing area.

Memorial Event for the 20 year anniversary of the attack on the twin towers.
