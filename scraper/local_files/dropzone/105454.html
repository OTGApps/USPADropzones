Flight - 1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses

  02/22/2020 - 02/23/2020

  Skydive City Zephyrhills   Zephyrhills Florida

   Stocum, Aaron M., Course Director
Aaron Stocum, Main Contact

 Aaron@Flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Aaron Stocum will be teaching Flight-1 101 & 102 canopy courses at Skydive City on the 22nd & 23rd of February 2020. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module, you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Cost per course is $150 plus 5 hop n pops. Flight 101 - Saturday 22 February Flight 102 - Sunday 23 February To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/4141 102 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/4142
