Pepperell Skydiving Center

  Pepperell Airport (26MA), Pepperell, MA

165 Nashua Rd
Pepperell MA 01463-1457 US

40 miles NW of Boston
5 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skyjump.com
  (978) 433-9222
  fran@skyjump.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 208

We are close to Boston and easy to reach from any New England metro area. New England Boogie with Skyvan. Free load organizers every weekend. Take Rt 3 to Exit 5W (Rt 111 West) in Nashua, N.H. Go west 5 miles to Pepperell Airport on the left. Only 15 minutes from the SkyVenture New Hampshire wind tunnel. Parachute shop and master rigger on airport. Open til October 31st.
