TNT Brothers Seaside

  Aeroportual International Tulza, Tuzla, Romania

Aeroportul International
Comuna Tuzla 907295 Romania

20 km S of Constanta
5 km to nearest hotel


  https://www.tnt-brothers.ro
  0040371100600
  office@tnt-brothers.ro
  open map
 
 

Open May to September-Friday to Sunday with possibility of extending programme on request. Base on Tusla International airport close to Tuzla and Costinesti beach, it is easily accessible from Constanta and from the main highway connecting the seaside to Bucharest, the capital city. Hangar with packing area, course and rigger rooms. Coaching available for formation skydiving, freefly, beginner, intermediate and performance canopy piloting. Nice bar and terrace just near the hangar and a beautiful restaurant nearby within the airport.
