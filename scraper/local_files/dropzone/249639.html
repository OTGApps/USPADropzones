Flight-1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses at Piedmont Skydiving

  10/02/2021 - 10/03/2021

  Piedmont Skydiving   Salisbury North Carolina

   Chasen McCook, Main Contact

 386-898-1932

 chasen@flight-1.com

 https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Chasen McCook will be teaching Flight-1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses at Piedmont Skydiving on the 2nd & 3rd of October 2021. “The Active Pilot” (101): Say hello to Joe Jumper, our fully animated, three dimensional, pilot! Joe, along with your Flight-1 Instructor, will introduce the fundamentals of canopy flight to ensure consistent, soft landings. Together, they will ensure you fully understand the correct flare technique and develop the ability to debrief personal performance. The course re-defines flight planning using modern techniques and tools, to give you confidence in your accuracy. “Developing Our Range” (102): Joe Jumper takes you through a series of in-air exercises to explore the complete range of the canopy. You will be given more safety and survival tools, including controlled turns using different inputs, dive recoveries and how to deal with a long spot. Experience and preparation for emergency situations in differing conditions is the key to this course. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 2 October Flight 102 - Sunday 3 October To join, sign up here: 101 - http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/5110 102 - http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/5111
