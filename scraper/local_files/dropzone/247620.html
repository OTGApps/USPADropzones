Skydive Windy City-Chicago

  Michigan City Municipal Airport (MGC), Michigan City, IN

1300 S Indiana Highway 212
Michigan City IN 46360-3186 US

52 miles E of Chicago
1 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivewindycitychicago.com
  (219) 210-3338
  skydive@skydivewindycitychicago.com
  open map
  Mid-Eastern Region
  1 PAC 750XL

Skydive Windy City is the closest skydiving center to Chicago. Located in NW Indiana, we offer amazing views of Lake Michigan and downtown Chicago. Jump altitiude is 10,000' (AGL). We are located at the Michigan City Municipal Airport (MGC), less than a 1 hour drive from downtown Chicago or the South Suburbs. We offer tandem jumping, solo training and experienced (licensed) jumping.
