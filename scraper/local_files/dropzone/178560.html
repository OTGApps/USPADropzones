Midwest Freefall Sport Parachute Club, Inc.

  Kuntsman Airfield, Ray, MI

62912 Kunstman Rd
Ray MI 48096-3050 US

20 miles NE of Detroit
7 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.midwestfreefall.com
  (586) 752-5867
  skydive@midwestfreefall.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 182, 1 Cessna Blackhawk Caravan

A small, casual DZ with a country atmosphere and nice turbine aircraft. Only a few miles from metro Detroit. Located just southeast of Romeo, Michigan, on the Kunstman Airfield at 29 Mile and Kunstman Rd. USPA membership required. Check website for hours and special events.
