  09/27/2019 - 09/27/2019

Cleveland Skydiving Center, Main Contact

 jump@clevelandskydivingcenter.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Kaz Sheekey will be teaching Flight-1 201 canopy course at Cleveland Skydiving Center on the 27th of September 2019. Module 200 provides a safe environment for skydivers wanting to progress their canopy skills to the next level in the area of high performance landings. **REQUIREMENT TO TAKE FLIGHT 201: Minimum of 200 jumps** We also highly recommend to have completed & attended Flight 101 and 102.
