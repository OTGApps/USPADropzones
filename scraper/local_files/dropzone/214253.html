FSC Suedpfalz E.V.

  Schweighofen Airport, Rheinland-Pfalz, Germany

Middle Härtel A
airfield Schweighofen 
Schweighofen 76889 Germany

140 km SW of Frankfurt
5 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.fsc-suedpfalz.de
  0049-176-631-372 82
  info@fsc-suedpfalz.de
  open map
 
  1 Dornier 27, 1 Pilatus Porter

Lovely dropzone in the southwest of Germany near the French border and the "Deutsche Weintor". Perfect for teams or solo jumpers. RW and freefly coaching available at a low price. Wingsuit rental and coaching available. Inside packing area, packers, gear rental, restaurant, videographers, team room and load organizing. Open from March to November, Friday - Sunday and in the week on inquiry. Camping and hotels just 5 km. away. Sleep possible in bunkhouse, please bring your airbed.
