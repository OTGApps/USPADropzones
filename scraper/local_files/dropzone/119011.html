Skydive Coastal Carolinas

  Cape Fear Regional Jetport (SUT), Oak Island, SC

4019 Long Beach Rd SE
Southport NC 28461-8654 US

25 miles S of Wilmington
2 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivecoastalcarolinas.com
  (910) 457-1039
  skydivecoastalcarolinas@yahoo.com
  open map
 
  2 Cessna 182, 1 King Air

Skydive Coastal Carolinas is located one mile from the Atlantic ocean offering a picturesque view from jump altitude. South of Wilmington, north of Myrtle Beach. Southport and Oak Island also offer a variety of activities along with lodging and restaurant within two miles. Regular beach jumps enjoyed at sunset (C-License holder or higher).
