Skydive Coastal Carolinas

  Cape Fear Regional Jetport (SUT), Oak Island, SC

4019 Long Beach Rd SE
Southport NC 28461-8654 US

25 miles miles   S of Wilmington
2 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivecoastalcarolinas.com
  (888) 899-JUMP
  bstrong53@gmail.com
  open map
  Mid-Atlantic Region
  2 Cessna 182, 1 King Air

Skydive Coastal Carolinas is located one mile from the Atlantic ocean offering a picturesque view from jump altitude. South of Wilmington, north of Myrtle Beach. Southport and Oak Island also offer a variety of activities along with lodging and restaurant within two miles. Regular beach jumps enjoyed at sunset (C-License holder of higher).
