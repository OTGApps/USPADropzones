Skydive Madrid

  Airfield of Senasa, Ocana-Madrid, Spain

Aerolibre SL-Aerodromo de Senasa
Centro de Paracaidismo- Ctra A4 KM 64 
Ocana Toledo 45300 Spain

62 km miles   S of Madrid
1 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivemadrid.es
  34 911 299 376
  info@skydivemadrid.es
  open map
  Foreign
  2 Pilatus Porter A34, 1 Cessna Caravan Blackhawk

Open seven days a week year round. 2 Pilatus Porters. 64 km from Madrid using free motorway A-IV. Hotel and restaurants located at the drop zone. We are one of the largest and most experienced drop zones in Spain. We are the closest drop zone to Madrid City offering a very friendly staff, shop, packers and riggers. Modern facility with a great night life.
