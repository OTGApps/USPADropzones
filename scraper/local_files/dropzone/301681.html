Skydive Balaton

  Siofok-Kiliti Airport, Siofok, Hungary

SioFok-Kiliti Repuloter
Szekszardi UT 
Siofok H-8611 Hungary

5 km miles   S of Siofok
0.5 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivebalaton.com
  36 70 433 2800
  info@skydivebalaton.com
  open map
  Foreign
  2 MI-8 Helicopters

DZ is near Siofok town at Lake Balaton; 1 hour traveling from Budapest. Season is from April 1 to November 1. DZ is open on weekends and for organized events. Each Mi-8 helicopter can accommodate up to 29 jumpers per load. There are restaurants, air-conditioned accommodations for up to 80 people and 9 hangars for packing. Balaton Lake is only 5 km away and has beach and party areas. Please contact in advance before arriving. For more details check www.skydivebalaton.com
