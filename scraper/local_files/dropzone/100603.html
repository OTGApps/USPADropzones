Module 200 provides a safe environment for skydivers wanting to progress their canopy skills to the next level in the area of high performance landings. **REQUIREMENT TO TAKE FLIGHT 201: Minimum of 200 jumps** We also highly recommend to have completed & attended Flight 101 and 102. Flight - 1 101 & 102 Canopy Courses

  06/27/2020 - 06/28/2020

  Aerohio Skydiving Center   Rittman Ohio

   Stocum, Aaron M., Course Director
Aaron Stocum, Main Contact

 Aaron@Flight-1.com

 http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Aaron Stocum will be teaching Flight 101 & 102 Canopy Courses at Aerohio Skydiving Center on the 27th & 28th of June 2020. Module 100 provides the cornerstone for safety & survival skills under canopy. Throughout this module you will learn the essential skills required for consistently accurate landings, various control inputs, how to deal with emergency situations and effectively managing air traffic. Flight 101 & 102 courses satisfy the requirements for USPA's Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card for your B license and go way above and beyond them. Flight 101 - Saturday 27 June Flight 102 - Sunday 28 June To join, sign up here: 101 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/4218 102 - https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/4219
