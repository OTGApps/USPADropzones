Skydive Salerno

  Salerno Costa d'Amalfi, Bellizzi, Italy

Areoportr Salerno Costa d'Amalfi
Via Olmo 
Bellizzi (SA) 84092 Italy

10km S of Salerno
3km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivesalerno.it
  3314112896
  info@skydivesalerno.it
  open map
 
  1 Pilatus Porter

Open every weekend, all year long. At 40 minutes from Naples Airport or 10 minutes from Salerno Train station. Pick up service for groups of 9 people from Naples Airport. Tandem, AFF and fun jumps. Jump from 4500 meters. Discount for hotels and B&B around the drop zone.
