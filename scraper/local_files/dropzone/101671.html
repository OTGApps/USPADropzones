Skydive Empuriabrava

  Empuriabrava Airport, Empuriabrava, Spain

Sector Aeroclub S/N
Empuriabrava 17487 Spain

150 km N of Barcelona
1 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveempuriabrava.com
  34(972)450111
  info@skydiveempuriabrava.com
  open map
 
  1 Beech 99, 1 Super Twin Otter, 1 Pilatus Porter

Open seven days a week all year. Situated 1.5 hours north of Barcelona on the Costa Brava. Just 2km from the Mediterranean Sea in Empuriabrava with many hotels, apartments, and restaurants within walking distance. Wind Tunnel and Team Training facilities on site. Coaching and training from basic to advanced levels offered. Note: All skydivers are required to use AADs.

Flight-1 201 Canopy Course at Skydive Empuriabrava

  08/22/2021 - 08/22/2021

  Skydive Empuriabrava   Empuriabrava Girona

   Pete Allum, Main Contact

 386-898-1932

 pete@flight-1.com

 https://flight-1.com/sport/certification/

Pete Allum will be teaching a Flight 201 Canopy Course at Skydive Empuriabrava on the 22nd of August 2021. “Managing Airspeed” (201) A must for all pilots regardless of experience! Inevitably all pilots at some point have to deal with different airspeeds close to ground. How will you react? Will you react correctly? The course highlights how different airspeeds are controlled safely for each situation and provides the know-how required to react correctly. This course is also a must if you intend to move into more advanced landings. **REQUIREMENT TO TAKE FLIGHT 201: Minimum of 200 jumps** We also highly recommend to have completed & attended Flight 101 and 102. 201 - Sunday 22 August To join, sign up here: 201: http://flight-1.com/sport/certification/courses/view/5353
