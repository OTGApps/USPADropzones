Skydive Finger Lakes

  Ovid Airport (D82), Ovid, NY

2562 Parish Rd
Ovid NY 14521-9540 US

25 miles NW of Ithaca
6 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivefingerlakes.com
  (315) 220-0052
  info@skydivefingerlakes.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 182

Open every day but Tuesday at the DZ-owned Ovid Airport. Located in the heart of the Finger Lakes region, with wineries, state parks, swimming, boating, hiking and fishing nearby. Skydivers of all levels and backgrounds are very welcome. Cook outs on Saturdays. No hook turns please.
