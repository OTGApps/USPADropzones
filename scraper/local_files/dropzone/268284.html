Skydive Long Island

  Brookhaven Airport (HWV), Shirley, NY

135A Dawn Dr
Shirley NY 11967-1946 US

50 miles E of New York City
4 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivelongisland.com
  (516) 656-5867
  jump@skydivelongisland.com
  open map
  Eastern Region
  2 Cessna 182

Open seven days per week seasonally April through November. From New York City, take the Long Island Expressway (LIE) east to Exit 68. Take William Floyd Pkwy south 2.2 miles to Dawn Dr. Turn left onto Dawn Dr. Go .5 miles to 135 Dawn Dr. Skydive Long Island is on the left in the main terminal building of the Brookhaven-Calabro Airport. Enjoy beautiful views of Long Island's world-renowned North and South Shores along with the Atlantic coastline and Hampton Beaches.
