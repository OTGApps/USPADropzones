Skydive Algarve

  Aerodromo Municipal De Portimao, Portimao-Alvor, Portugal

Aerodromo Municipal de Portimao
Montes de Alvor 
Portimao-Alvor 8500-059 Portugal

5 km W of Alvor-Portimao
500 mtrs to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivealgarve.pt
  351-282-496581
  info@skydivealgarve.com
  open map
 
  2 Dornier G92, 1 Cessna 208 Super Caravan

Skydive Algarve operates fast-climbing turbine aircraft to 15,000ft over the beautiful beaches of the Portuguese Algarve region, 365 days a year. Based on a municipal airfield, the DZ facilities are ideal for those looking for a family friendly sun, sea & skydiving location. Jumps on to the local beach is a highlight for many skydivers who attend. Always contact the DZ to register your visit.
