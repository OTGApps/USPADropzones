Sky Down Skydiving

  Caldwell Industrial Airport (EUL), Caldwell, ID

5111 Aviation Way
Caldwell ID 83605-1500 US

15 miles W of Boise
2.2 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydownskydiving.com
  (208) 455-2359
  jumpskydown@gmail.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 182, 1 Cessna 182 Super-Charged

Open 365 days a year. A friendly, yet professional environment where safety always comes first. Huge facility with easy I-84 access. 2500 foot indoor climate controlled packing room. Large grass landing area for experienced jumpers. Multiple landing areas to accommodate all skill levels. Breathtaking view of Lake Lowell, Boise valley, the Snake River and Owyhee Mountains. Come jump with us for one of the most scenic views in Idaho!
