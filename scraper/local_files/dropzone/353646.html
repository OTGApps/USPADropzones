Skydive Madagascar

  Arivonimamo Aeroport, Ambatomirahavavy, Madagascar

Arivonimamo - Centre De Parachutisme
Ambatonmirahavavy - Madagascar

45 km W of Antananarivo
to nearest hotel


  http://skydive-madagascar.com
  +261344877777
  skydive.madagascar@gmail.com
  open map
 
  Cessna 182, Cessna 206

Tandem activity, skydiving training. Open every weekend and week by reservation for parachuting courses. Skydive Madagascar is located in Arivonimamo on the old international airport, 45 km from Antananarivo. C182 and C206 for jumps at 10,000 feet. Skydive Madagascar is the only parachuting center recognized and authorized to practice the parachuting activity by the Parachute Federation of the country. Next activity on the whole of the big island. Join us on site: Arrived at Arivonimamo, just after Jovena station, take the track on the left for 2 km. Follow the signs. Accommodation in the center will be available soon. Contact us for more information.
