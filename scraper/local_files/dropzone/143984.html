Veterans Wind Therapy

  08/28/2021 - 08/28/2021

  Wisconsin Skydiving Center   Jefferson Wisconsin

   Andy Van Handel, Main Contact

 920-470-9071

 badandysta@gmail.com

 www.veteransskydive4life.org

August 28, 2021 Veterans Skydive 4 Life is hosting its annual Veterans Wind Therapy Event at Wisconsin Skydiving Center in Jefferson, WI with the goal of taking 10 veterans on skydives to help them cope with PTSD, and reduce veterans suicide, and show them what life is about.
