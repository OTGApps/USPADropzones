740 Grand St

We are headquartered in Allegan Michigan and offer a full range of skydiving services. We offer first-time tandem skydives, complete student skydiving training, and lots of fun jumps for experienced, licensed skydivers. We are located less than an hour away from Grand Rapids, Lansing, Battle Creek, Kalamazoo and the Michigan lakeshore. Come see what all the excitement is all about!
