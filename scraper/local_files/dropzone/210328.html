Skydive Allegan

  Padgham Field (35D), Allegan, MI

740 Grand St
Allegan MI 49010-8013 US

30 miles S of Grand Rapids
2 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveallegan.com
  (269) 998-7747
  jump@skydiveallegan.com
  open map
 
  2 Cessna 182

We are headquartered in Allegan Michigan and offer a full range of skydiving services. We offer first-time tandem skydives, complete student skydiving training, and lots of fun jumps for experienced, licensed skydivers. We are located less than an hour away from Grand Rapids, Lansing, Battle Creek, Kalamazoo and the Michigan lakeshore.
