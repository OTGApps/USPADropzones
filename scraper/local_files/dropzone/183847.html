Skydive Mesquite

  Mesquite Municipal Airport (67L), Mesquite, NV

1200 Kitty Hawk Dr Ste 105
Mesquite NV 89027-3220 US

77 miles N of Las Vegas
1/2 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivemesquite.com
  (877) 2GO-JUMP
  skydive@skydivemesquite.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 182

Located one hour northeast on I-15 from Las Vegas and 1/2 hour south from St. George, Utah. Caravan all winter. Indoor packing area. Open all weekends. Weedays by appointment. Freefly, Wingsuit or RW, all welcome. If you want to fun jump near Las Vegas, this is the place to be.
