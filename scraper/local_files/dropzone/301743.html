  PK Airpark (5W4), Raeford, NC

143 Airport Dr
Raeford NC 28376-8087 US

14 miles SW of Fayetteville
4 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveparacletexp.com
  (910) 904-0000
  manifest@skydiveparacletexp.com

  1 Cessna 208 Grand Caravan, 4 CASA 212, 1 Twin Otter, 1 Skyvan

Open year round, 7 days a week, sunrise to sunset at PK Airpark in Raeford, NC. 2 Grand Caravans, 1 Twin Otter, 1 Supervan and 2 CASA's available. Swoop pond, enclosed packing and full-service bar and grill. Convenient to Fayetteville, Raleigh, and Southern Pines. Five minutes from Paraclete XP SkyVenture Indoor Skydiving.
