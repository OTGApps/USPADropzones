Skydive Gananoque

  Gananoque Municipal Airport, Gananoque, Canada

258 Airport Rd
Gananoque ON K7G 2V3 CANADA

35 km NE of Kingston
10 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivegan.com
  (613) 382-5114
  info@skydivegan.com
  open map
 
  1 C-182, 1 C-206, 1 King Air

Located in the scenic Thousand Islands area of Eastern Ontario. Skydive Gananoque (pronounced GAN-AN-OCK-WAY) is mid-way between Montreal and Toronto and a 20 minute drive from the Canada/U.S. border of upstate New York. Open seven days a week April -October. Coaching available on weekends (freestyle, style & accuracy and RW).
