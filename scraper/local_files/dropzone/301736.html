Skydive Costa Rica

  Boca Naranjo Airport, Quepos, Costa Rica

Boca Naranjo Airport
Quepos - Costa Rica

5 km SE of Quepos
5 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivecostarica.com
  506-84068544
  info@skydivecostarica.com
  open map
  Foreign
  1 Cessna 206, 1 Cessna 182

Open every weekend and some days during the week. We have a river, natural pool, restrooms and houses close by and available for rent. Coaching available in all disciplines. AFF available as well. From Caballo Blanco on the Interamerican Hwy, 400m south, go 4km and turn right onto a gravel road. Go 100m and turn right again. Gate will be on your right.
