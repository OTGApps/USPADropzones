ParisJump

  Aerodrome de St Florentin Cheu, Cheu, France

Route de L'Aerodrome
Cheu 89600 France

170 km SE of Paris
On-Site to nearest hotel


  http://www.parisjump.com
  33(3)658637799
  contact@parisjump.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna Caravan

ParisJump is one of the nearest DZ's to Paris (170 km). AFF facilities are available on weekends and one week a month. We are located in the Bourgogne region with is famous for its bourguignon beef and wine (Charlis is approx. 15 min away). Load organizers are available. See you soon!
