Skydive Northstar

  Waseca Municipal Airport (ACQ), Waseca, MN

35493 110th St
Waseca MN 56093-4520 US

60 miles SW of Twin Cities
2 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivenorthstar.com
  507-291-5867
  info@skydivenorthstar.com
  open map
 
  2 Cessna 182

Skydive Northstar is a part-time seasonal drop zone. Hours of operation are primarily weekends (Saturday and Sunday) and weekdays vary (check website and Facebook page for details). Skydive Northstar offers USPA licensing packages (IAD) , tandem, coaching and welcomes licensed skydivers. Located off HWY 14 (approx. 60 miles SW of the Twin Cities.
