Southern Minnesota Skydiving, LLC

  Waseca Municipal Airport (ACQ), Waseca, MN

35493 110th St
Waseca MN 56093-4520 US

60 miles SW of Twin Cities
2 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.southernminnesotaskydiving.com
  507-461-5838
  info@southernminnesotaskydiving.com
  open map
  North Central Region
  2 Cessna 182s

Southern MN Skydiving is a part-time seasonal drop zone. Hours of operation are primarily weekends (Saturday and Sunday) and weekdays vary (check website and Facebook page for details). SMS offers USPA licensing packages (IAD & SL) , tandem, coaching and welcomes licensed skydivers. Located off HWY 14 ( approx. 60 miles SW of the Twin Cities).
