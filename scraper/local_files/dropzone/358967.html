Skydive Costa D'Argento

  Aviosuperficie Costa D'Argento, Orbetello, Italy

Strada Prov.Le Parrina
Albinia-Orbetello Grpssetp 58015 Italy

130 km N of Rome
1 km to nearest hotel


  https://www.skydivecostadargento.com
  +39 339 723 5967

Between the sea and the salt lagoon of Monte Argentario, on the seaside if Tuscany. 25 km south from Grosseto and 130 km north from Rome. Easily reachable along SS-1 also from Pisa Airport and Rome Airport. Coaching available in all desciplines. Load Organizer always available. Twice a month big events are usually scheduled with Special Guests.
