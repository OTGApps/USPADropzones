Meadow Peak Skydiving

  Carson Field (MT53), Marion, MT

10260 Lost Prairie Rd
Marion MT 59925 US

39 miles W of Kalispell
5 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.meadowpeakskydiving.com
  (406) 858-2330
  mpskydiving@gmail.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 182

Meadow Peak Skydiving opened in 2011. We have gradually expanded to include a new hanger, shower house and manifest building. We host the annual Lost Prairie Boogie in August. This year, we are offering tandem jumps and student training throughout the jumping season.
