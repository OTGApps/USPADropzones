5 km to nearest hotel

Niagara Skydive Centre is a full-time skydive facility from May to October. First class facilities and training, located a short distance from the Greater Toronto area. Friendly atmosphere with free camping and show facilities. We operate a Navajo (10 passenger) and Cessna for your skydiving needs. Very scenic view!!!
