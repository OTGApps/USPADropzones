Seven Hills Skydivers of Madison, WI

  Mathaire Field (WI97), York Center, WI

7530 State Road 73
Marshall WI 53559-9017 US

15 miles N of Madison
5 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.sevenhillsskydivers.org
  (920) 623-2838
  mail@sevenhillsskydivers.org
  open map
 
  2 Cessna 182

Located 15 minutes north of Madison on a rural, picturesque Mathaire Field airport. Friendly, non-profit club owned and operated. Established in 1962. Large, comfortable clubhouse with indoor heated and air-conditioned packing area. Great camping and picnic areas on the DZ. Open Wednesday afternoons in summer (4-sunset) and weekends (9-sunset) April thru October.
