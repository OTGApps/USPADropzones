New York Tandem Skydiving

  Greenwood Lake Airport, West Milford, New Jersey

126 Airport Rd
West Milford NJ 07480-4603 US

30 mi N of New York City metro area
13 mi to nearest hotel


  https://www.newyorktandemskydiving.com
  (973) 659-4736
  newyorktandemskydiving@gmail.com
  open map
 
 

New York Tandem Skydiving offers a great view of New York City Skyline at 120 miles per hour. We are the closest skydiving center to New York City and we specialize in first time tandem skydiving. Located at the Greenwood Lake Airport with a great airport restaurant and friendly staff. Call or book online to schedule your skydive with amazing views, 120 mph thrills and friendly instructors.
