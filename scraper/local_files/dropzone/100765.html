AXIS B-License Canopy Course and Water Training Canopy Course

  08/31/2019 - 08/31/2019

  Skydive Arizona   Eloy Arizona

   Niklas Daniel, Main Contact

 (520)466-4200

 Info@AXISFlightSchool.com

 http://www.AXISFlightSchool.com

The AXIS B-License Day will be taught by AXIS Flight School coach Niklas Daniel at Skydive Arizona. This intensive one day course satisfies the USPA B-License Canopy Piloting Proficiency Card requirements. Above all else, we seek to improve your understanding, confidence and skill as a canopy pilot. Written Exam and assistance with the license application process is available. Cost per course participant is $200 plus 5 hop&pop tickets. Hiring a packer for the day is required. For those who wish to participate in water training, the cost is an additional $50, and will be made available at the end of the day. Sign up here: http://www.axisflightschool.com/CCD_11_12.php Questions? Contact us at Info@AXISFlightSchool.com
