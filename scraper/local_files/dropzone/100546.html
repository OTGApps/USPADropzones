I am conducting this USPA Coach Course as part of a prototype 10-day tandem instructor professional training program that will be held at Skydive Deland. The coach course may be attended by anyone that meets the coach course prerequisites. Earning a tandem rating post-course is not required. Tandem Instructor Rating Course (UPT)

  08/25/2020 - 08/30/2020

  Skydive DeLand, Inc.   Deland FL

   Noonan, Thomas J., Course Director
Tom Noonan, Main Contact

 (603) 714 4382

 skydiveearth@gmail.com

This is a six-day USPA/UPT Tandem Instructor rating course aimed at the tandem instructor candidate that intends to pursue tandem instruction as a full time career. A traditional tandem instructor course runs approximately 3 to 4 days and consists of 10 skydive/evaluation jumps. This course will run 6 full-days and cover the entire spectrum of professional development as it relates to being a full time tandem instructor. The course will consist of 20 skydives: 10 evaluation skydives, as well as 10 additional tandem skydives that will cover multiple aircraft exits and performance standards in varying conditions and configurations. This course is intended to prepare it's graduates with a course of study that will give them a fully immersive experience in tandem training and performance standards. For information on the prerequisites to earn a USPA/UPT Tandem Rating Course, please review USPA IRM T-1: A.4.
