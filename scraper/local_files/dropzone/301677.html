Timisoara Parachute Club Sportiv

  Charlie Bravo Airfield , Siria, AR, Romania

Aerodrom Siria DN709
Siria 317340 Romania

28 km NE of Arad
2 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.gojump.ro
  407 73787553
  office@gojump.ro
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 172

Timisoara is Romainia's 3rd largest city, and this new DZ is located within easy reach. We are open to all jumpers on weekends, public holidays and occasionally midweek. Take the DN 709 28 km out of Arad. The DZ is on your left just before the beautiful hill town of Siria.
