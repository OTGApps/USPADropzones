Leteck 1
Loosava 262/1 
Prostejov 73601 Czech Republic

60 km N of Brno
5 km to nearest hotel

Organizer of the World Championships 2014 and 2018, European Championships 2005,2009,2012, World Cup 2009,2012, and the Vector Festival 2001,2012. Free DZ transportation from Brno Intl. Airport. Open on weekends; 7 days a week during the summer. 4000m (13130ft) from 25.5. Coaching available in FF, FS, WS and SU. Please check our website for more information.
