Skydive New England

  Skydive New England Airport (ME64), Lebanon, ME

40 Skydive Ln
Lebanon ME 04027-4359 US

65 miles N of Boston
5 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivenewengland.com
  (207) 339-1520
  info@skydivenewengland.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 206, 1 Twin Otter

Open five days a week (TH-M) from May01 to November01. Flying a Super Twin Otter and Cessna 206. On-site camping, private airport, bunkhouses, showers/bathrooms, covered packing, video rooms and on-site restaurant. Offering Tandem and AFF training as well as FF and RW organizing. Plus! The only swoop pond in New England!
