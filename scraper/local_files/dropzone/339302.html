Skydive Put-in-Bay

  Put-in-Bay Airport, Put-in-Bay, Ohio

1494 Langram Rd
Put In Bay OH 43456-6721 US

22 miles S of Sandusky
1 mile to nearest hotel


  http://skydivepib.com
  419-573-2960
  skydivepib@gmail.com
  open map
  Mid-Eastern Region
  1 Piper PA 32-300

Open May 15th - October 31. Tandem only drop zone located at Put-in-Bay Airport on South Bass Island. A small island in Lake Erie with plenty of extra entertainment. Just north of Cedar point amusement park. Take Miller Ferry or Jet Express to reach the island.
