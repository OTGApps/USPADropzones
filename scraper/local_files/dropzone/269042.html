Skydive Chelan

  Lake Chelan Airport (S10), Chelan, WA

201 Airport Way
Chelan WA 98816-9145 US

40 miles N of Wenatchee
3 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivechelan.com
  (509) 881-0687
  info@skydivechelan.com
  open map
  Northwest Region
  1 Cessna 182

We are open May-Sept 5 days a week, closed Tuesday & Wednesday. During the Fall and Spring we are open weekends. Our facilities consist of snack bar, refreshments, indoor packing area and outdoor lawn. We are a C182 dropzone with occasional helicopter and turbine aircraft. We specialize in first time tandem skydives and advanced flying skills. We have on sight Instructor Examiner for Tandem rating, Coach rating, and Static Line rating. Current USPA membership required.
