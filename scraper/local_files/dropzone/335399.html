Dallas Tandem Skydiving

  Ennis Municipal Airport (F41), Ennis, TX

100 Airport Dr
Ennis TX 75119 US

30 miles S of Dallas
3 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.dallastandemskydiving.com
  214-838-0111
  dallasareaskydiving@gmail.com
  open map
  Southwest Region
 

Open seven days a week and only 30 minutes from Dallas. Enjoy a beautiful scenic flight on every tandem skydive, followed by 30-40 seconds of freefall and a 5-7 minute parachute ride. Dallas Tandem Skydiving offers personalized service for each customer. You can also enjoy recreational activities like fishing and horseback riding in the vicinity of Dallas Tandem Skydiving. Call today to schedule your first skydive.
