Skydive Zanzibar

  Kigunda Aerodrome, Nungwi, Zanzibar

Kendwa Beach Hotel
Kendwa - Tanzania

2 kms S of Zanzibar City
1 km to nearest hotel


  https://www.skydive-zanzibar.com
  +255-776-200200
  jump@skydive-zanzibar.com
  open map
 
 

Skydive Zanzibar is located on the east coast of Africa. It is open seven days a week for Tandems and AFF courses. Fun Jumpers always welcome, please coordinate with us as we also have a Caravan available for larger groups. We are located at Kendwa Rocks Hotel, amazing Accommodations and full facilities available.
