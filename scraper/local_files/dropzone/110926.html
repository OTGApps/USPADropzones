Ogden Skydiving Center

  Ogden-Hinckley Airport (OGD), Oden, UT

3463 Airport Rd
Ogden UT 84405-2690 US

30 miles N of Salt Lake City
3 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveogden.com
  (801) 627-JUMP
  info@skydiveogden.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 182, 1 Cessna 208

DZ is just off I-15. Operations are typically Thursday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday. Please call for jump times. We fly over the beautiful Rocky Mountains and have awesome sunsets over the Great Salt Lake. Very competitive with gear sales.
