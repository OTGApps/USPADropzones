Ogden Skydiving Center

  Ogden-Hinckley Airport (OGD), Oden, UT

3463 Airport Rd
Ogden UT 84405-2690 US

30 miles N of Salt Lake City
3 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveogden.com
  (801) 627-JUMP
  info@skydiveogden.com
  open map
  Mountain Region
  1 Cessna 182, 1 Cessna 208

DZ is just off I-15. Operations are typically Friday, Saturday, Sunday and Monday. Please call for jump times. We fly over the beautiful Rocky Mountains and have awesome sunsets over the Great Salt Lake. Occasional boogie schedule during summer season. Very competitive with gear sales.
