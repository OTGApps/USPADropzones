  Narnaul Airport, Narnaul, India

Dhanipur Airstrip, NH-11
Panethi Village 
Narnaul - India

140 km SW of NARNAUL
6 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skyhighindia.com
  91 9968007331
  info@skyhighindia.com
  open map
 
  2 Cessna 172

Open 5 days a week, just 2.5 hours away from National Capital New Delhi. We use a brand new 206H and Cessna 172. AFF programs run every month. We have a very big landing area for students and beginners. Limited gear for rent, there is a gear store available and we are also dealers for Sunpath and Vectors rigs. We have a cafe on site which serves hot meals, snacks and beverages through the day.
