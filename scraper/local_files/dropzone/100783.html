Coach Rating Course

  03/21/2020 - 03/23/2020

  Skydive Santa Barbara, LLC   Lompoc CA

   Mongoven, Casey P., Course Director
Casey Mongoven, Main Contact

 (760) 987-1187

 casey@freefalldatasystems.com

 https://www.facebook.com/events/646120142826000/

Freefall Data Systems CEO Casey Mongoven is holding a USPA Coach Rating Course at Skydive Santa Barbara from March 21st to 23rd, 2019. Price is $300 plus the cost of jump tickets (at least 4 @ $26 a piece). Candidates must have previously observed and assisted in one solo first jump course, completed 100 skydives and have a B License, and answered at least 80% of the questions on the USPA Coach Final Examination correctly. Candidates are expected to have read the entire portion of the Instructor Rating Manual pertaining to the Coach Rating prior to attending the course. The current Instructional Rating Manual can be found here: https://uspa.org/IRM. Candidates should have rigs with reserves in date or they will have to rent gear. Contact FDS for questions and to register.
