Skydive Angola

  Aeroporto de Caboledo, Caboledo, Angola

Aeroporto Militar Caboledo
Caboledo Angola

120 km S of Aeroporto de Caboledo
to nearest hotel


 
  244925071856
  Skydiveangola@gmail.com
  open map
 
  2 turboprops

Located in Caboledo, the heart of the Natural Park paradise surf spot and beach resort. We are a boutique size operation dedicated to strong personal experiences and we fly our 2 turboprops aircrafts mostly on weekends. We have a 3km runway and a have an obstacle free landing zone. Several restaurants and beach facilities rest 5 minutes from the DZ. In a nutshell, you can do a safari, ride waves and still make the sunset load.
