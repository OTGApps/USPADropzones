Start Skydiving

  Middletown Regional Airport (MWO), Middletown, OH

1711 Run Way
Middletown OH 45042-2300 US

30 miles N of Cincinnati
0.5 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.startskydiving.com
  (513) 422-5867
  info@startskydiving.com
  open map
 
  2 Cessna 208 Caravan, 1 Cessna 206

Amenities include: - 10,000 square feet of indoor hangar space complete with heat, cooling fans, insulation, rig racks, storage lockers, and a carpeted and padded indoor packing space. This hangar space is secured with multiple 24/7 cameras and is locked and armed with an alarm system every night. - online self-manifesting - air-conditioned and heated classrooms, restrooms, and registration/manifest area that are all fully finished - free wireless internet access - over 200 acres of level, soft, cut sod to land on airport property - rental gear - world-class coaching from Team Fastrax - debriefing stations - creeping area - mockups - load organizers - a grill serving food, a gear store - 24/7 access to showers and restrooms - shaded campgrounds with a fire pit and large picnic area with grills - Direct TV in lounge area - an RV park with electric hookups - a long bike path along the river - kayaking - a family park with splash pad right next to the airport DZ open seven days a week. Visit website for more info.
