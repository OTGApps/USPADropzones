Skydive Madera

  Madera Municipal Airport (MAE), Madera, CA

4130 Aviation Dr Ste D
Madera CA 93637-9442 US

17 miles N of Fresno
0.8 mile to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivemadera.com
  (559) 674-1278
  sara@skydivemadera.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna 182

Open Saturdays and Sundays year round. Small and super friendly atmosphere, everyone welcome. From Hwy 99 exit Ave 17 and go west to Airport Dr. Follow to the 4-way stop, turn south on Aviation Dr. and go to the 3rd to last hanger. Call if the gate is closed.
