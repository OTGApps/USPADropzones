Skydive Spaceland-Atlanta

  Spaceland Airport (Private), Rockmart, GA

1195 Grady Rd
Rockmart GA 30153-3919 US

45 miles NW of Atlanta
5 miles to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydivespaceland.com
  (770) 748-2200
  manifestga@skydivespaceland.com
  open map
 
  1 Cessna Caravan

Freefall over the scenic hills of Georgia just 45 miles northwest of downtown Atlanta! Our completely remodeled facility is open everyday- except Christmas-on our own private airfield. We have state-of-the-art gear and offer training to become a skydiver in as little as a week! We fly a Super Caravan daily. Also, convenient to Birmingham, AL and Chattanooga, TN.
