Skydive Portugal

  Aerodromo Municipal de Evora, Evora, Portugal

Aerodromo Municipal de Evora
Estrada de Viana Do Alentejo-ER254 
Evora PC 7005-210 Portugal

4 km S of Evora
3.5 km to nearest hotel


  http://www.skydiveportugal.pt
  351-910-999-991
  info@skydiveportugal.pt
  open map
 
  2 Cessna Caravans 208

Skydive Portugal is an experienced drop zone and school dedicated to professional and fun skydiving. Flying 2 Grand Caravans and a Cessa 206, open year-round and strategically located in Evora-the UNESCO capital of Alentejo. Located only 50 min away from Lisbon by car, 220km from Faro, and 400km from Oporto. Skydive Portugal is already the reference for skydivers all around the world! From seasonal boogies to special events, at Skydive Portugal all disciplines are welcome. Instruction and coaching are available from our highly trained and friendly staff.
